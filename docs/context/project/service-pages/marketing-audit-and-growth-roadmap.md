## H1 direction
Marketing audit and growth roadmap for businesses that need clarity before they need more execution.

## Plain-English definition
This service diagnoses what is working, what is not, and what should happen next so the business can stop guessing.

## Why it matters
Without a clear order of operations, teams waste time and money fixing the wrong thing first.

## Signs you need it
- too many priorities and no sequence
- traffic, leads, CRM, and website all feel partially broken
- marketing spend exists but confidence is low
- leadership cannot clearly say what the next 90 days should focus on

## What it usually includes
- channel and offer review
- website and conversion review
- CRM / automation / reporting review
- problem diagnosis
- ranked priority roadmap
- implementation recommendation

## Technical scope
Funnel review, systems audit, conversion-path review, reporting gap analysis, and practical sequencing.

## Proof block
Use the CMO Roadmap Generator and CMO Simulator as method proof, then connect to Graston as execution proof after diagnosis.
