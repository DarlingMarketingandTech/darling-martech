# Lead Generation Systems

## Page role
Explain this service as the bridge between demand, capture, follow-up, and qualification.

- **Priority:** 2
- **Primary audience:** SMB founder, operator, sales-led service business
- **Primary CTA:** `/contact?intent=service`
- **Primary proof:** `/work/graston-technique`
- **Supporting proof:** `/work/investment-roi-planner`, `/work/barbershop-command-center`

## H1 direction
Lead generation systems that connect attention, capture, and follow-up into one working path.

## Plain-English definition
This service improves how the business turns traffic, interest, or outreach into qualified leads and usable next steps.

## Why it matters
Leads are often lost not because demand is missing, but because the capture and follow-up system is weak.

## Signs you need it
- lead volume is inconsistent
- forms and offers are weak or generic
- follow-up is slow or unreliable
- lead quality varies wildly
- marketing and sales are not aligned on what happens next

## What it usually includes
- offer/capture review
- form and intake-path review
- qualification logic
- follow-up structure
- handoff design between marketing and sales

## Technical scope
Lead routing, form strategy, qualification logic, CRM handoff, and top-of-funnel system structure.

## Proof block
Use Graston Technique as the headline outcome case; support it with Investment ROI Planner and Barbershop Command Center as mechanism proofs.

## FAQ targets
- is this more marketing or more systems work?
- can this improve lead quality, not just volume?
- do I need a new CRM for this?
- can this be improved without rebuilding the whole site?
