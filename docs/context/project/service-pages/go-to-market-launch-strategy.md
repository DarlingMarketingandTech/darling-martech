- **Primary CTA:** `/contact?intent=service`

## H1 direction
Go-to-market and launch strategy for businesses that need a rollout plan, not just activity.

## Plain-English definition
This service structures how a new offer, campaign, or initiative goes to market so attention turns into action.

## Why it matters
Weak launches waste timing, messaging, and internal momentum.

## Signs you need it
- a new offer is coming but rollout is unclear
- audience and channel choices are fuzzy
- launch work feels reactive instead of planned
- the team lacks a cohesive sequence

## What it usually includes
- audience and message alignment
- launch sequencing
- offer and channel planning
- landing/conversion path direction
- rollout timeline and support assets

## Technical scope
GTM sequencing, campaign architecture, audience logic, landing-page requirements, and launch measurement planning.

## Proof block
Use 317 BBQ as the primary commercial rollout proof. Support with Circle City Kicks or Graston where launch and campaign sequencing mattered.
