# Marketing Strategy & Consulting

Most consultants hand you off to a team the moment you sign. I work with you directly. Strategy and execution in one person. No account manager layer. No hand-offs to juniors. You get the senior strategist doing the work.

## The Problem Most Businesses Have

You've probably tried a few things:

- Hired an agency. They promised results. You got a junior on your account and an inflated bill. Or the strategy worked for a month, then stopped. They moved on to the next client.
- Built it in-house. Your team is smart. But marketing strategy + marketing ops + analytics + tech stack is three full-time jobs. You're stretched thin.
- Pieced together tools and freelancers. You've got Salesforce, HubSpot, Google Ads, a freelance designer, a part-time copywriter. Nobody talks to each other. You talk to six different people.

The result: your marketing generates activity, not revenue. You spend money. You don't know why it worked or why it stopped.

## What This Actually Looks Like

### Phase 1: Audit & Strategy (4-8 weeks)

I dig into what you're doing now:

- Current marketing infrastructure: where leads come from, where they drop off, what you're tracking vs. not tracking
- Your tech stack: CRM, email, analytics, ads, automation — how they talk to each other
- Your team and resources: who's doing what, what you can build in-house, what you need help with
- Your market: who you're really talking to, what they actually care about, why they choose you or your competitors

From that, I build a strategy that sits in reality. Not a 50-page deck nobody reads. A document that answers: Where should I focus your business? How do I measure it? What should I build first? What can wait?

### Phase 2: Build & Execute (Ongoing)

This varies based on what you need:

- **Marketing infrastructure.** Rebuild your CRM workflows so leads flow where they need to go automatically. Set up automation that actually saves you time. Build email sequences that don't feel like automation.
- **Go-to-market.** New product, new market, new positioning. I help you figure out who actually cares, how to reach them, what to say so they listen.
- **Campaign strategy.** You have a product or service. Sales is slow. I audit the entire path from awareness to close — ads, landing page, email, product experience — and find where the leak is.
- **Content systems.** Content is marketing strategy you publish. I help you figure out what kind of content actually works for your business, how to produce it consistently, and how to distribute it so people actually see it.
- **Pricing & positioning.** Sometimes the problem isn't marketing. It's that you're underpriced, or nobody understands what you do, or you're trying to be everything to everyone. I help you figure that out and rebuild the story.

You're never waiting for me to finish my part and hand it off. I'm working in parallel with you and your team.
