## H1 direction
Internal tools and AI workflows for teams that need faster decisions, less repetition, and cleaner internal processes.

## Plain-English definition
This service builds lightweight internal systems that help people make repeated decisions faster and with more consistency.

## Why it matters
When knowledge lives in people, not systems, support load increases and scale gets harder.

## Signs you need it
- staff repeat the same lookup or decision tasks constantly
- support questions pile up
- process knowledge is trapped in a few people
- work depends too heavily on manual judgment

## What it usually includes
- workflow discovery
- decision-tree or rules logic
- internal tool design
- lightweight AI assistance where useful
- process-support system design

## Technical scope
Internal app architecture, rules engines, AI-assisted workflows, client-side tools, and lightweight process systems.
