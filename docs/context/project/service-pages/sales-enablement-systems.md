# Sales Enablement Systems

## Page role
Position this as the systems layer that helps sales teams qualify faster, quote faster, and move the right opportunities forward.

- **Priority:** 2
- **Primary audience:** founder-led sales team, high-consideration offer, small B2B or service sales org
- **Primary CTA:** `/contact?intent=service`
- **Primary proof:** `/work/smart-sales-pricing`
- **Supporting proof:** `/work/investment-roi-planner`

## H1 direction
Sales enablement systems that make qualification, quoting, and sales conversations easier to run.

## Plain-English definition
This service builds the tools and structures that help a sales process move faster and with more clarity.

## Why it matters
When qualification and quoting are inconsistent, lead quality suffers and sales slows down.

## Signs you need it
- quoting is manual or slow
- qualification criteria are fuzzy
- prospects ask the same early questions repeatedly
- sales reps handle similar situations differently

## What it usually includes
- sales-process review
- qualification logic
- quoting/pricing tool strategy
- support tools and calculators
- handoff and decision support

## Technical scope
Calculator/planner structure, pricing logic, qualification tooling, sales-support workflows, and internal or buyer-facing enablement tools.

## Proof block
Use Smart Sales & Pricing Tool as the primary proof and Investment ROI Planner as supporting top-of-funnel/sales-support proof.
