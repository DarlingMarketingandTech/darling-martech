# Content & Demand Systems

## Page role
Position content work as a structured demand asset, not random publishing.

- **Priority:** 3
- **Primary audience:** founder, operator, SEO-aware business with inconsistent or unstructured content
- **Primary CTA:** `/contact?intent=service`
- **Primary proof:** `/work/behr-pet-essentials`
- **Supporting proof:** `/work/primary-colours`, `/work/graston-technique`

## H1 direction
Content and demand systems for businesses that need their content to support growth, trust, and conversion.

## Plain-English definition
This service turns scattered content efforts into a system that helps the business get found, explain itself clearly, and support demand over time.

## Why it matters
Content without structure rarely compounds. It should support search, trust, and conversion together.

## Signs you need it
- content is irregular or reactive
- the site does not answer real buyer questions
- publishing exists but outcomes are weak
- no system connects service pages, FAQs, and supporting content

## What it usually includes
- content-system planning
- search-intent mapping
- FAQ/service support structure
- editorial and asset prioritization
- conversion-aware content direction

## Technical scope
Content architecture, internal linking, search-intent mapping, editorial structure, and conversion alignment.

## Proof block
Use Behr Pet Essentials as the primary content-system example. Support with Primary Colours and Graston where content clearly supported visibility or engagement.
