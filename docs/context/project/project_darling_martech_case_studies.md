## 1. Graston Technique

### Core Summary

- Client / problem:
  - Graston Technique had a static directory, fragmented training pipeline, disconnected systems, and heavy manual operational overhead.
- What was broken:
  - the provider ecosystem was not functioning like a growth engine
  - enrollment, lifecycle, and support workflows were fragmented
  - analytics and attribution were unclear
  - infrastructure and operations were carrying unnecessary risk and overhead
- What was built:
  - a full MarTech ecosystem across learning, commerce, CRM, workflows, analytics, AI-assisted systems, and infrastructure hardening
- Measurable outcomes:
  - `+212% qualified leads`
  - `95% overhead reduction`
  - `48 hrs/wk saved`
- Services involved:
  - CRM, Automation & RevOps
