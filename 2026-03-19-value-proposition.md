> Last Updated: 2026-03-19 | Owner: Jacob Darling

## The Core Problem

Most small and mid-sized businesses have tried marketing help. They've hired agencies that specialize in one thing, freelancers who execute without strategy, or generalist consultants who deliver a deck and leave. The result is always the same: fragmented work, unclear ROI, and a founder who still feels like they're guessing.

The real problem isn't that good marketing talent doesn't exist. It's that **strategy and technology almost never travel together.** Marketing people can't build the systems. Technical people don't understand positioning. And when you split those jobs across multiple vendors, accountability disappears in the gap between them.

---

## The Darling MarTech Value Proposition

**Darling MarTech gives growing businesses what agencies can't: senior-level marketing strategy and the technical execution to actually implement it — under one roof, with one person accountable for the result.**

This isn't a full-service agency. It's not a freelancer. It's not a fractional CMO who hands off execution. It's the combination of all three disciplines, from someone with 15+ years of proof across healthcare, SaaS, legal, finance, e-commerce, and more.

## Why This Matters: The ROI Frame

**73% of SMBs lack confidence in their marketing strategies.**
**Only 30% of CMOs can confidently measure marketing ROI.**

These aren't abstract statistics. They describe what almost every SMB founder experiences. They spend money on marketing, hope it works, and can't prove whether it did. That uncertainty drives bad decisions: cutting budgets that were working, keeping vendors who weren't, and building on foundations that have no data under them.

Darling MarTech solves this by treating measurement as a first-class deliverable. Before any campaign launches, we define what success looks like, what we'll track, and how we'll know if it's working. That's not a nice-to-have — it's the foundation of accountable marketing.

## What Makes This Different

**1. One person owns everything.**
Strategy, implementation, technology, reporting. No account manager in the middle. No hand-offs where things get lost. One person, fully accountable.

**2. Senior-level thinking, not account management.**
The person who scopes the work is the same person who builds it and reviews the results. That's rare at any price point. It's almost nonexistent in the SMB market.

**3. Technical depth that most marketers don't have.**
Jacob can build a Next.js web application, configure a CDP, architect a marketing automation system, and write the analytics queries to measure it. That's not standard. That combination is what makes the integrated model real — not just a positioning claim.

**4. Proof over promise.**
Specific, auditable results across multiple industries. Not vague directional language. Not agency-speak. Real numbers from real engagements.

**5. No agency overhead.**
No account manager fees, no project management layers, no creative director markups. The budget goes to the work, not the administration of the work.
